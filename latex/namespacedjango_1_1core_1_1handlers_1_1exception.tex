django.\+core.\+handlers.\+exception.\+convert\+\_\+exception\+\_\+to\+\_\+response (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{}]{get\+\_\+response}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})}

\begin{DoxyVerb}Wrap the given get_response callable in exception-to-response conversion.

All exceptions will be converted. All known 4xx exceptions (Http404,
PermissionDenied, MultiPartParserError, SuspiciousOperation) will be
converted to the appropriate response, and all other exceptions will be
converted to 500 responses.

This decorator is automatically applied to all middleware to ensure that
no middleware leaks an exception and that the next middleware in the stack
can rely on getting a response instead of an exception.
\end{DoxyVerb}
