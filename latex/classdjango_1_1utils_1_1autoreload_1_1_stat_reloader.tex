django.\+utils.\+autoreload.\+Stat\+Reloader.\+tick (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{}]{self}{}\end{DoxyParamCaption})}

\begin{DoxyVerb}This generator is called in a loop from run_loop. It's important that
the method takes care of pausing or otherwise waiting for a period of
time. This split between run_loop() and tick() is to improve the
testability of the reloader implementations by decoupling the work they
do from the loop.
\end{DoxyVerb}
